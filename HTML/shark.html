----The Hammerhead Shark/Sphyrnidae----

About me :D

The Hammerhead Shark is a species of shark with 9 different subspecies roaming mostly in the Atlantic, Indian and Pacific Ocean but can be found in the Mediterranean sea. These sharks spend their time in 74 - 80 F C water eating stringrays, other rays and skates.

Facts:

Diet:
Stringrays, other rays and skates
Location
Atlantic, Indian and Pacific Ocean and also in the Mediterranean sea
Average Lifespan:
20 to 30 years
Predators:
None

Fun Fact:

Hammerheads have eyes that allow 360 degrees of vision but cannot see in front of them.
