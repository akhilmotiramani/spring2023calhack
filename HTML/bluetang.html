----The Blue Hippo Tang/Paracanthurus hepatus----

About me :D

The Blue Hippo tang is a eye-catching fish often found in the Western Atlantic Ocean from New York all the way to the Gulf of Mexico, and then south to Brazil. Much like the Clownfish the majority of the Tangs diet comes from algae. The Blue Tang has a very unique skill that helps him hide from its preditors such as the tuna, bar jack, tiger grouper, and other large carnivours fishes, it can turn invisible(semi-transparent)

Facts:

Diet:
Algae
Location
Western Atlantic Ocean (New York --> Gulf of Mexico --> Near Brazil
Average Lifespan:
12 to 15 years
Predators:
tuna, bar jack, tiger grouper, and other large carnivours fishes

Fun Fact:

the Blue Hippo Tang can turn semi-transparent to blend in with its surroundings and avoid dangerous predators.
